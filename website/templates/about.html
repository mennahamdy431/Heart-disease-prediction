About Heart Diseases

Heart disease, also known as cardiovascular disease, is a term that encompasses a range of conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels. These conditions include:

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart failure
  • Heart valve disease
  • Arrhythmias

Heart disease is the leading cause of death for both men and women worldwide. However, there are steps you can take to reduce your risk of developing heart disease:

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke
  • Control your blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Manage stress

Tips for Heart Disease Patients

If you have been diagnosed with heart disease, there are additional steps you can take to manage your condition and improve your overall health:


  • Take your medications as prescribed
  • Follow a heart-healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly, with your doctor's approval
  • Quit smoking if you haven't already
  • Limit alcohol intake
  • Get enough sleep
  • Manage stress
  • Stay up-to-date on your doctor appointments and checkups
